§ i · course

AI-Assisted Development.

A course on using AI to build webapps, algorithms and infrastructure — and the tools and techniques to maximise efficiency.

The landscape of software development has shifted permanently. AI tools can compress months of learning, accelerate debugging and code review, and free up cognitive bandwidth for the hard design decisions. This course walks through the practical side — not the hype.

The goal is to make you genuinely productive with AI tools across the full development lifecycle: from spinning up a webapp, to algorithm design, to shipping and maintaining infrastructure. Every technique is grounded in real workflow patterns that compound over time.

  • Building full-stack web applications with AI assistance
  • Algorithm development, debugging and optimisation
  • Infrastructure as code and cloud deployment workflows
  • Tool selection — editors, agents, APIs and prompt strategies
  • Effective prompting and context management for developers
  • Efficiency patterns that scale with team size and project complexity

§ ii · mentoring

Mentoring & Training.

Short, focused sessions — from one-off problem solving to structured mentoring for software developers at any stage.

Technical skills compound fastest with the right guidance at the right moment. Whether you're stuck on an architecture decision, learning a new domain, or mapping out a career path — a focused session with someone who has navigated the terrain shortens the path considerably.

Sessions are scoped to what you actually need: an hour to unblock a specific problem, a short intensive on a topic, or ongoing mentoring over weeks or months. The format adapts to where you are and where you're going.

  • One-on-one mentoring — monthly package or ad hoc sessions
  • Short intensive training on specific topics or tools
  • Code review, architecture critique and design feedback
  • Career path guidance for engineers and developers
  • Problem-solving sessions and collaborative debugging
